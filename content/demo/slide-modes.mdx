## Expandable

Click each item to reveal its detail.

<Expandable>
  <ExpandableItem title="What is an expandable?">
    A native `<details>` element styled with Tailwind. Free accessibility, free state.
  </ExpandableItem>
  <ExpandableItem title="When should I use one?">
    For optional supporting detail that distracts from the main point if always-on.
  </ExpandableItem>
  <ExpandableItem title="Are multiple items independent?">
    Yes. Each one toggles on its own; opening one does not close the others.
  </ExpandableItem>
</Expandable>

## Reveal

Press `Space` to reveal the next bullet. After the last one, `Space` advances to the next slide.

<Reveal>
  <RevealItem>**First** — visible on slide entry.</RevealItem>
  <RevealItem>**Second** — appears on the first Space press.</RevealItem>
  <RevealItem>**Third** — appears on the second Space press.</RevealItem>
  <RevealItem>**Fourth** — appears on the third Space press, after which Space advances slides.</RevealItem>
